Foundations

Developmental Program

Our developmental track introduces essential soccer skills in a fun, engaging way — building the foundation for travel-level play.

What players experience

For younger age groups, our training staff runs a tournament-based developmental program that puts the fundamentals first. Players learn the joy of the game while quietly building the toolkit that lets them step up to league-level play when the time is right.

  • ✓ Small-group, age-appropriate training
  • ✓ Ball mastery, footwork, & first-touch development
  • ✓ Tournament-based competitive experience
  • ✓ Pathway into our 2026–27 Travel Teams

Year-Round Development

Training that doesn’t stop when the season ends.

Our players keep getting better when other teams pack up. SWR FC builds on the EDP season with indoor work all winter long and dedicated speed & agility sessions.

Winter

Indoor Training at Futsal631

All winter long, our teams train indoors at Futsal631. Futsal’s small-sided, high-touch format sharpens decision-making, ball control, and tight-space technique — the skills that show up most when players return to the outdoor field in spring.

Year-Round

Speed & Agility Training

Dedicated speed, agility, and athletic-development sessions built into our calendar. Faster first steps, better change-of-direction, and the conditioning to outlast opponents in the second half.
