How It Works

Three simple steps

1

Pick your team

Click Register on your child’s team above. You’ll land on our official LeagueApps portal.

2

Complete the form

Sign in (or create a free LeagueApps account) and fill in your player’s information.

3

Choose how to pay

Pay in full, or pick the Monthly plan — 4 equal payments on the 15th, August through November.

Your roster spot is confirmed once registration and the first payment are complete. You’ll get a confirmation email from LeagueApps right away.
