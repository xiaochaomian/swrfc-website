How It Works

Three simple steps

1

Pick your team

Click Register on your child’s team above. You’ll land on our official LeagueApps portal.

2

Complete the form

Sign in (or create a free LeagueApps account) and fill in your player’s information.

3

Secure your spot

Submit your registration — you’ll get a confirmation email from LeagueApps right away, and your roster spot is set.
