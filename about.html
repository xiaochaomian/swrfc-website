About SWR FC

A local club with a national-level approach to player development.

Our Story

Developing confident players, right here at home.

Shoreham-Wading River FC has built a reputation for offering some of the most competitive scholastic soccer programs on Long Island. Our community has invested heavily in player development, and every year our athletes and coaches continue to grow together.

Our goal is simple: keep talented players local by offering a higher-quality experience at an affordable price — right in their own backyard.

Why SWR FC

What sets us apart

★ Local-First

Train and play close to home with coaches who know your community. No long commutes, no big-club price tags.

★ High-Level Coaching

Our staff is committed to building on the fundamentals players already have, integrating advanced techniques and team strategies.

★ Real Development Pathways

From developmental to travel-level competition, we build players who are ready to perform at the highest levels of youth soccer.

★ Character on & off the Field

We emphasize respect, courtesy, and teamwork — preparing coachable athletes who thrive as supportive teammates.

Our Approach

Comprehensive training for every age and skill level.

SWR FC offers programs designed to grow with players — from first-touch fundamentals through competitive EDP league play, with indoor training all winter at Futsal631 and dedicated speed & agility sessions to round out the year.
